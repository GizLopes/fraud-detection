Fraud Detection Business Rules

Evaluate the transaction risk considering:

1. High transaction amount increases risk
2. High transaction velocity in 24h increases risk
3. Device changes increase risk
4. Country mismatch between merchant and IP increases risk
5. High-risk merchants increase risk
6. Very recent customers increase risk
7. Card-not-present transactions increase risk

Classification categories:

- LEGIT
- SUSPICIOUS
- FRAUD

You must:
- reason over the signals
- estimate the overall fraud risk
- classify the transaction

Return JSON format:

{
  "classification": "LEGIT | SUSPICIOUS | FRAUD",
  "reasoning": "short explanation",
  "risk_score": 0-100
}
